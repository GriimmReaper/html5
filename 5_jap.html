Soufflé Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Milk - 1/2 cup
  • All-purpose flour - 1/2 cup
  • Granulated sugar - 2 tablespoons
  • Baking powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt - a pinch
  • Butter - for cooking
  • Powdered sugar - for serving
  • Maple syrup - for serving
  • Fresh berries - for garnish (optional)
  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk the egg yolks and milk until combined. Sift in the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt, and mix until smooth.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  3. Gently fold the beaten egg whites into the egg yolk mixture in thirds, being careful not to deflate the mixture.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over low heat and lightly grease with butter.
  5. Using a ring mold or a metal cookie cutter, place it in the skillet and spoon the batter into the mold, filling it about halfway. Cover with a lid and cook for 3-4 minutes, then flip and cook for another 2-3 minutes, or until the pancakes are cooked through.
  6. Remove the ring mold and serve the pancakes warm, dusted with powdered sugar, and drizzled with maple syrup. Garnish with fresh berries if desired.
