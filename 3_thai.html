Thai Chili Broccoli Salad Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Broccoli - 1 large head, cut into florets
  • Red bell pepper - 1, thinly sliced
  • Carrots - 2, peeled and thinly sliced or julienned
  • Red onion - 1/2 small, thinly sliced
  • Fresh cilantro leaves - 1/2 cup, chopped
  • Roasted peanuts - 1/4 cup, chopped (optional)
  • Fresh Thai basil leaves - 1/4 cup, chopped (optional)
  • For the dressing:
  • Lime juice - 3 tablespoons
  • Soy sauce or tamari - 2 tablespoons
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Fresh red chili - 1, minced (adjust to taste)
  1. In a small bowl, whisk together lime juice, soy sauce (or tamari), minced garlic, minced red chili, brown sugar (or coconut sugar), salt, and oil to make the dressing. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  2. Blanch broccoli florets in boiling water for 1-2 minutes until tender-crisp. Drain and rinse under cold water to stop cooking. Pat dry with a towel.
  3. In a large bowl, combine blanched broccoli florets, sliced red bell pepper, sliced carrots, sliced red onion, and chopped cilantro.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well to coat all ingredients evenly.
  5. Let the salad sit for 10-15 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  6. Before serving, garnish with chopped roasted peanuts and Thai basil leaves if using.
  7. Serve chilled or at room temperature. Enjoy!
