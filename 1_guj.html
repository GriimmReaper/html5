Sev Tameta Sabji Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Tomatoes - 3-4 medium, chopped
  • Sev - 1 cup, fried chickpea flour noodles
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
  • Cumin seeds - 1 tsp
  • Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Red chili powder - 1 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 tbsp
  • Salt - to taste
  • Sugar - 1 tsp (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves - for garnish
  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add asafoetida (hing), turmeric powder, and chopped tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
  3. Add red chili powder, coriander powder, salt, and sugar (if using). Mix well and cook for a few more minutes.
  4. Add fried sev and mix gently so that sev gets coated with the tomato mixture.
  5. Remove from heat. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  6. Serve hot with chapati or puri.
