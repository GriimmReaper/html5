Zaru Soba Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Soba noodles - 8 ounces (about 230 grams)
  • Dipping Sauce (Tsuyu):
  • - Soy sauce - 1/2 cup
  • - Mirin - 1/4 cup
  • - Water - 2 cups
  • Garnishes (optional):
  • - Nori seaweed, shredded
  • - Wasabi, grated
  • - Green onions, thinly sliced
  • - Shichimi togarashi (Japanese seven spice)
  1. Boil soba noodles in a large pot of water according to package instructions, usually about 5-7 minutes, until tender but firm (al dente).
  2. Drain the noodles and rinse under cold water to remove excess starch and cool them down. Drain well again.
  3. Prepare the dipping sauce (tsuyu): In a saucepan, combine soy sauce, mirin, and water. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, then remove from heat and let it cool.
  4. Divide the dipping sauce into individual serving bowls.
  5. Arrange cooled soba noodles on a bamboo or wire rack (zaru) to let excess water drip off.
  6. Optional: Garnish with shredded nori seaweed, grated wasabi, sliced green onions, and shichimi togarashi for extra flavor.
  7. To eat, take a small bundle of soba noodles and dip into the tsuyu sauce. Enjoy immediately.
