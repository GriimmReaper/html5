Akha Rigna Bateta nu Shak Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Potatoes (Akha Rigna) - 4 medium, boiled and peeled
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Mustard seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch
  • Green chilies - 2, slit
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Jaggery - 1 tablespoon, grated (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves - for garnish
  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add asafoetida (hing), slit green chilies, and turmeric powder. Saute for a few seconds.
  3. Break the boiled potatoes into rough pieces and add them to the pan. Mix gently to coat the potatoes with the spices.
  4. Add red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. If using jaggery, add it now. Mix and cook on low heat for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the potatoes are well coated and heated through.
  6. Remove from heat. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot with roti or paratha. Enjoy!
