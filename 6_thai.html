Mixed Tofu Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Firm tofu - 1 block (about 14 oz or 400g), cut into cubes
  • Broccoli florets - 2 cups
  • Red bell pepper - 1, thinly sliced
  • Snow peas - 1 cup, trimmed
  • Carrot - 1, julienned
  • Spring onions (scallions) - 4, chopped
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1 tablespoon, minced
  • Soy sauce or tamari - 3 tablespoons
  • Rice vinegar - 2 tablespoons
  • Sesame oil - 1 tablespoon
  • Sriracha or chili garlic sauce - 1 teaspoon (optional)
  • Sesame seeds - 1 tablespoon, for garnish
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and minced ginger. Stir-fry for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add cubed tofu to the pan. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tofu is golden brown and crispy on all sides.
  4. Push the tofu to one side of the pan and add broccoli florets, red bell pepper, snow peas, julienned carrot, and chopped spring onions.
  5. Stir-fry vegetables for 3-4 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce (or tamari), rice vinegar, sesame oil, and sriracha (or chili garlic sauce).
  7. Pour the sauce over the tofu and vegetables. Toss gently to combine everything evenly.
  8. Continue stir-frying for another 1-2 minutes until everything is heated through.
  9. Remove from heat and transfer to a serving dish.
  10. Garnish with sesame seeds.
  11. Adjust seasoning with salt if needed.
  12. Serve hot over steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy your Mixed Tofu!
