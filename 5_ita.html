Bruschetta alla Caprese Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Fresh baguette or ciabatta - 1 loaf, sliced into 1/2-inch slices
  • Ripe tomatoes - 4 large, diced
  • Fresh mozzarella - 8 ounces, diced
  • Fresh basil - 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Olive oil - 1/4 cup
  • Balsamic vinegar - 1 tablespoon (optional)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - to taste
  • Garlic - 1 clove, peeled (for rubbing on the bread)
  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Arrange the bread slices on a baking sheet and brush them lightly with olive oil.
  2. Toast the bread in the oven for about 5-7 minutes, or until golden and crispy. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly.
  3. While the bread is toasting, prepare the topping: In a bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and chopped basil. Drizzle with olive oil and add salt and black pepper to taste. Optionally, add balsamic vinegar for extra flavor.
  4. Once the bread is slightly cooled, rub one side of each slice with the peeled garlic clove for a subtle garlic flavor.
  5. Top each toasted bread slice with a generous spoonful of the tomato and mozzarella mixture.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with extra basil if desired.
