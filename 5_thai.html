Rocky Pad Thai Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Rice noodles - 8 oz (about 225g), soaked in hot water until softened
  • Firm tofu - 8 oz (about 225g), cubed
  • Bean sprouts - 1 cup
  • Green onions - 4, chopped
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Red bell pepper - 1, thinly sliced
  • Carrot - 1, julienned
  • Roasted peanuts - 1/2 cup, chopped
  • Fresh cilantro leaves - 1/2 cup, chopped
  • For the sauce:
  • Tamarind paste - 2 tablespoons, dissolved in 1/4 cup warm water
  • Soy sauce or tamari - 3 tablespoons
  • Brown sugar or palm sugar - 2 tablespoons
  • Fresh lime juice - 2 tablespoons
  • Sriracha or red chili flakes - 1 teaspoon (optional)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and chopped green onions. Stir-fry for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add cubed tofu to the pan. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tofu is golden brown and crispy.
  4. Add sliced red bell pepper and julienned carrot. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  5. Add soaked rice noodles to the pan. Toss gently to combine with the vegetables and tofu.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together tamarind paste mixture, soy sauce (or tamari), brown sugar (or palm sugar), fresh lime juice, sriracha (or red chili flakes), and salt.
  7. Pour the sauce over the noodles and vegetables. Toss well to coat evenly.
  8. Continue stir-frying for 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through and well combined.
  9. Remove from heat and stir in bean sprouts.
  10. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  11. Transfer to serving plates or bowls.
  12. Garnish with chopped roasted peanuts and fresh cilantro leaves.
  13. Serve hot with lime wedges on the side. Enjoy your Rocky Pad Thai!
