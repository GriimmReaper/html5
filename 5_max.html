Nachos Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Tortilla chips - 1 bag (about 10-12 ounces)
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar or Mexican blend) - 1 1/2 cups
  • Black beans - 1/2 cup, drained and rinsed
  • Jalapeno peppers - 2, sliced (optional)
  • Tomatoes - 1 medium, diced
  • Red onion - 1/4 cup, finely chopped
  • Fresh cilantro - 2 tablespoons, chopped
  • Guacamole or avocado - 1, mashed (optional)
  • Sour cream - for serving (optional)
  • Salsa - for serving (optional)
  • Black olives - sliced, for garnish (optional)
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread tortilla chips evenly on a baking sheet.
  3. Sprinkle shredded cheese over the chips.
  4. Add black beans, jalapeno peppers, diced tomatoes, and chopped red onion evenly over the cheese.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. Remove from oven and let cool slightly.
  7. Top with chopped cilantro, guacamole or mashed avocado, sour cream, salsa, and sliced black olives if desired.
  8. Serve immediately as a delicious appetizer or snack.
