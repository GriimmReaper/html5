Tacos Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Ground beef - 1 lb (450g)
  • Yellow onion - 1 medium, diced
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Taco seasoning - 1 packet (or 2 tablespoons homemade)
  • Tomato paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Refried beans - 1 can (15 oz), optional
  • Taco shells - 12
  • Shredded lettuce - 2 cups
  • Diced tomatoes - 1 cup
  • Shredded cheddar cheese - 1 cup
  • Sour cream - 1/2 cup
  • Salsa - for serving
  • Avocado slices - for garnish (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro - for garnish (optional)
  1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add diced onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook until onions are translucent.
  3. Stir in taco seasoning and tomato paste. If using refried beans, add them now and mix well.
  4. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through.
  5. Meanwhile, heat taco shells according to package instructions.
  6. Fill each taco shell with the meat mixture.
  7. Top tacos with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream, salsa, avocado slices, and fresh cilantro.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Tacos!
