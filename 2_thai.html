Vegetarian Thai Red Curry Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Thai red curry paste - 3 tablespoons
  • Coconut milk - 1 can (400 ml)
  • Extra-firm tofu - 200 grams, cubed
  • Mixed vegetables (bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, etc.) - 2 cups, chopped
  • Bamboo shoots - 1/2 cup, sliced (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves - 1/2 cup, chopped
  • Kaffir lime leaves - 2, torn into pieces
  • Vegetable broth or water - 1 cup
  • Soy sauce or tamari - 1 tablespoon
  • Brown sugar or coconut sugar - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add Thai red curry paste and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add tofu cubes and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until lightly browned.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and vegetable broth (or water). Stir well to combine.
  5. Add mixed vegetables, bamboo shoots (if using), soy sauce (or tamari), brown sugar (or coconut sugar), and salt. Mix well.
  6. Simmer uncovered for 10-15 minutes or until vegetables are tender and the curry has thickened slightly.
  7. Remove from heat and stir in chopped basil leaves and torn kaffir lime leaves.
  8. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  9. Serve hot over steamed jasmine rice or noodles. Enjoy!
