Caponata Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Olive oil - 1/4 cup
  • Onion - 1 medium, chopped
  • Celery - 2 stalks, chopped
  • Red bell pepper - 1, chopped
  • Tomatoes - 2 large, diced (or 1 can crushed tomatoes)
  • Capers - 2 tablespoons, drained
  • Green olives - 1/4 cup, sliced
  • Raisins - 1/4 cup
  • Red wine vinegar - 2 tablespoons
  • Granulated sugar - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - to taste
  • Fresh basil - for garnish (optional)
  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet or pan over medium heat. Add the diced eggplant and cook until softened and slightly browned, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the eggplant and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add a bit more olive oil if needed, and saut√© the chopped onion, celery, and red bell pepper until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes (or crushed tomatoes), capers, green olives, and raisins to the skillet. Stir to combine.
  4. Return the cooked eggplant to the skillet. Stir in the red wine vinegar and sugar. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, until the vegetables are tender and the flavors meld together. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  5. Remove from heat and let the caponata cool to room temperature. Garnish with fresh basil before serving if desired.
  6. Serve the caponata as a side dish, on crostini, or as a topping for grilled meats or fish.
