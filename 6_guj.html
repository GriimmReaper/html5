Undhiyu Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Surati papdi (Valor papdi) - 1 cup, stringed and chopped
  • Potatoes - 2, peeled and cubed
  • Sweet potatoes - 1 small, peeled and cubed
  • Eggplant (brinjal) - 1 small, cubed
  • Raw bananas - 1 small, peeled and cubed
  • Small baby potatoes - 4-6
  • Fresh pigeon peas (tuvar dana) - 1/2 cup
  • Fresh coconut, grated - 1/2 cup
  • Ginger-green chili paste - 1 tablespoon
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Coriander-cumin powder - 1 tablespoon
  • Garam masala powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Sugar - 1 tablespoon
  • Tamarind pulp - 2 tablespoons
  • Salt - to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves - for garnish
  • Oil - 4 tablespoons
  1. Prepare all the vegetables as mentioned and keep them ready.
  2. Heat oil in a large heavy-bottomed pan or a pressure cooker.
  3. Add ginger-green chili paste and saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add all the cubed vegetables and fresh pigeon peas. Mix well.
  5. Add turmeric powder, coriander-cumin powder, garam masala powder, sugar, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Cover and cook on medium heat for 5 minutes.
  7. Add grated coconut and tamarind pulp. Mix well.
  8. If using a pressure cooker, close the lid and cook for 2 whistles. If using a pan, cover and cook until all vegetables are tender, stirring occasionally.
  9. Once done, check for seasoning and adjust if needed.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  11. Serve hot with puri or roti. Enjoy!
