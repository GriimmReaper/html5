Gujarati Recipes

Dhokla

Khandvi

Ingredients: Chickpea flour, yogurt, turmeric, green chilies, ginger, grated coconut, mustard seeds, cilantro.

Instructions: Cook chickpea flour, yogurt, and water into a paste. Spread thin layers, roll, and cut. Fry mustard seeds, green chilies, and curry leaves; pour over khandvi. Garnish with coconut and cilantro.

Undhiyu

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables (eggplant, potatoes, beans), green garlic, ginger, cilantro, coconut, spices.

Instructions: Mix vegetables with spices. Cook with green garlic, ginger, and cilantro. Garnish with coconut and serve hot.

Handvo

Ingredients: Rice, lentils, yogurt, bottle gourd, spices, sesame seeds.

Instructions: Soak rice and lentils; blend into batter. Add yogurt, bottle gourd, and spices. Bake into a cake; garnish with sesame seeds.

Gujarati Kadhi

Ingredients: Yogurt, gram flour, ginger, green chilies, curry leaves, mustard seeds.

Instructions: Mix yogurt and gram flour into a batter. Cook with spices. Fry mustard seeds, green chilies, and curry leaves; pour over kadhi.

Patra

Ingredients: Colocasia leaves, gram flour, tamarind, jaggery, spices, sesame seeds.

Instructions: Spread gram flour mix on colocasia leaves; roll and steam. Fry sesame seeds, tamarind, jaggery, and spices. Pour over patra and serve.
