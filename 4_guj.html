Khaman Dhokla Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Besan (Gram flour) - 1 cup
  • Sooji (Semolina) - 1 tablespoon
  • Curd - 1/2 cup
  • Water - 1/2 cup
  • Green chilies - 2, finely chopped
  • Ginger paste - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Sugar - 1 teaspoon
  • Eno fruit salt - 1 teaspoon
  • Oil - 1 tablespoon
  • Mustard seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Fresh coriander leaves - for garnish
  • Grated coconut - for garnish (optional)
  1. In a mixing bowl, combine besan, sooji, curd, water, chopped green chilies, ginger paste, salt, and sugar. Mix well to form a smooth batter.
  2. Grease a dhokla plate or any flat steaming vessel with oil.
  3. Add eno fruit salt to the batter and mix gently. The batter will become frothy.
  4. Immediately pour the batter into the greased plate and spread it evenly.
  5. Steam the batter for 12-15 minutes on medium heat or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  6. Once done, remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  7. For tempering, heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds and curry leaves. Let them crackle.
  8. Pour the tempering over the steamed dhokla.
  9. Cut dhokla into squares or diamonds. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and grated coconut (if using).
  10. Serve khaman dhokla warm with green chutney or tamarind chutney. Enjoy!
