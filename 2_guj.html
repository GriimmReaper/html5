Dahi ni Tikhari Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Plain yogurt (dahi) - 2 cups
  • Gram flour (besan) - 1 tablespoon
  • Oil - 1 tablespoon
  • Mustard seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Asafoetida (hing) - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Green chili, finely chopped - 1
  • Turmeric powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Red chili powder - 1 teaspoon (adjust to taste)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Jaggery - 1 tablespoon (optional)
  • Fresh coriander leaves - for garnish
  1. Whisk together plain yogurt (dahi) and gram flour (besan) in a bowl until smooth. Gradually add water to achieve a pourable consistency without lumps.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Once they start to crackle, add asafoetida (hing) and chopped green chili. Saute for a few seconds.
  3. Reduce the heat to low. Carefully add the yogurt mixture to the pan, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, salt, and jaggery (if using). Mix well and cook on low heat until the mixture comes to a gentle boil.
  5. Meanwhile, prepare the tadka (tempering): Heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them crackle. Add curry leaves and saut√© briefly until fragrant.
  6. Pour the hot tadka over the prepared yogurt curry (Dahi ni Tikhari). Garnish with fresh chopped coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice or Gujarati bhakri (roti made from millet flour). Enjoy!
