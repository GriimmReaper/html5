Vegan Laab Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Textured vegetable protein (TVP) - 1 cup, rehydrated
  • Shiitake mushrooms - 1 cup, finely chopped
  • Red onion - 1 small, finely chopped
  • Green onions - 2 stalks, finely chopped
  • Fresh mint leaves - 1/2 cup, chopped
  • Fresh cilantro leaves - 1/2 cup, chopped
  • Lime juice - 3 tablespoons
  • Roasted rice powder - 2 tablespoons
  • Thai chili flakes - 1 teaspoon (adjust to taste)
  • Soy sauce or tamari - 2 tablespoons
  • Sugar - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  1. Rehydrate TVP in hot water according to package instructions.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped red onion and saut√© until translucent.
  4. Add chopped shiitake mushrooms and cook until they release their juices.
  5. Add rehydrated TVP, soy sauce (or tamari), and Thai chili flakes. Cook for 5-7 minutes until TVP absorbs flavors.
  6. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  7. In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked TVP mixture with chopped green onions, mint leaves, cilantro leaves, lime juice, roasted rice powder, sugar, and salt. Mix well.
  8. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  9. Garnish with additional herbs if desired.
  10. Serve warm or at room temperature. Enjoy!
