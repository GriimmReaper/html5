Fried Green Tomatoes Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Green tomatoes - 4 medium, firm
  • Cornmeal - 1 cup
  • All-purpose flour - 1/2 cup
  • Egg - 1 large, beaten
  • Milk - 1/2 cup
  • Salt - 1 tsp, or to taste
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp, freshly ground
  • Vegetable oil - for frying
  1. Wash and slice the green tomatoes into 1/4 inch thick slices.
  2. In a shallow bowl, mix cornmeal, flour, salt, and pepper.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together egg and milk.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Dip each tomato slice into the egg mixture, then dredge in the cornmeal mixture, ensuring it's coated evenly.
  6. Place the coated tomato slices in the hot oil, frying in batches for about 3-4 minutes per side or until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Repeat with remaining tomato slices.
  9. Serve hot as an appetizer or side dish.
