Khandvi Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Besan (Gram flour) - 1 cup
  • Curd - 1 cup
  • Water - 1 cup
  • Ginger paste - 1 teaspoon
  • Green chili paste - 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Mustard seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Sesame seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Fresh coriander leaves - for garnish
  1. In a mixing bowl, combine besan, curd, water, ginger paste, green chili paste, turmeric powder, asafoetida, and salt. Mix well to form a smooth batter.
  2. Grease a large plate or tray with oil.
  3. Pour the batter into a non-stick pan and cook over low heat, stirring continuously, until it thickens and leaves the sides of the pan (about 10-12 minutes).
  4. Quickly spread a thin layer of the batter onto the greased plate using a flat spatula. Let it cool slightly.
  5. Cut the cooled batter into thin strips using a knife.
  6. Roll each strip carefully to form a cylindrical shape. Arrange the rolled khandvis on a serving plate.
  7. For tempering, heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds and sesame seeds. Let them splutter. Add curry leaves and saut√© for a few seconds.
  8. Pour the tempering over the arranged khandvis. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  9. Allow the khandvis to cool completely before serving. Serve with green chutney.
