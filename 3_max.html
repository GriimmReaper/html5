Burritos Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Flour tortillas - 4 large
  • Ground beef - 1 lb (450g)
  • Onion - 1 medium, chopped
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Black beans - 1 can (15 oz), drained and rinsed
  • Cooked rice - 1 cup
  • Shredded cheddar cheese - 1 cup
  • Salsa - 1/2 cup
  • Sour cream - 1/2 cup
  • Chopped fresh cilantro - 1/4 cup
  • Ground cumin - 1 teaspoon
  • Chili powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Vegetable oil - 1 tablespoon
  1. In a large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and minced garlic. Cook until onions are translucent.
  3. Add ground beef, ground cumin, chili powder, and salt. Cook until beef is browned and cooked through.
  4. Stir in black beans and cooked rice. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until heated through.
  5. Warm flour tortillas in a microwave or skillet according to package instructions.
  6. Divide the beef and bean mixture evenly among the tortillas.
  7. Top each tortilla with shredded cheddar cheese, salsa, sour cream, and chopped cilantro.
  8. Roll up each tortilla tightly to form a burrito.
  9. Serve immediately, whole or sliced in half, with additional salsa and sour cream on the side if desired.
