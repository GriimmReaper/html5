Tsukemono Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Japanese cucumbers - 4 small, thinly sliced
  • Sea salt - 2 tbsp
  • Rice vinegar - 1/4 cup
  • Sugar - 2 tbsp
  • Water - 1/4 cup
  • Red chili flakes (optional) - 1/2 tsp
  • Sesame seeds - 1 tbsp, toasted (optional)
  1. Place the thinly sliced cucumbers in a bowl and sprinkle with sea salt. Toss gently to coat evenly. Let sit for about 30 minutes.
  2. Rinse the cucumbers under cold water to remove excess salt. Drain well and pat dry with paper towels.
  3. In a small saucepan, combine rice vinegar, sugar, water, and red chili flakes (if using). Heat over medium heat until the sugar dissolves completely.
  4. Remove the vinegar mixture from heat and let it cool to room temperature.
  5. Transfer the cucumbers to a clean glass jar or container. Pour the cooled vinegar mixture over the cucumbers, ensuring they are fully submerged.
  6. Optional: Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds over the top for added flavor.
  7. Cover the jar or container and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight, to allow the flavors to meld.
  8. Serve chilled as a refreshing side dish or condiment.
