Quesadilla Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Flour tortillas - 4 large
  • Shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or a blend) - 2 cups
  • Black beans - 1/2 cup, drained and rinsed
  • Corn kernels - 1/2 cup
  • Red bell pepper - 1/2 cup, diced
  • Green onions - 2, chopped
  • Chopped cilantro - 2 tablespoons
  • Ground cumin - 1 teaspoon
  • Chili powder - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt - to taste
  • Vegetable oil or butter - for cooking
  • Sour cream - for serving (optional)
  • Salsa - for serving (optional)
  • Guacamole - for serving (optional)
  1. In a large bowl, combine shredded cheese, diced , black beans, corn kernels, diced red bell pepper, chopped green onions, chopped cilantro, ground cumin, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  3. Place one flour tortilla in the skillet. Sprinkle a generous amount of the cheese mixture evenly over the tortilla.
  4. Top with another tortilla.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the tortilla is golden brown and the cheese is melted, pressing down gently with a spatula.
  6. Remove quesadilla from the skillet and let it cool for a minute before cutting into wedges.
  7. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and cheese mixture.
  8. Serve hot, with sour cream, salsa, and guacamole on the side if desired. Enjoy your delicious Quesadillas!
