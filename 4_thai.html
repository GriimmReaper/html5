Stir-Fried Greens Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Assorted greens (spinach, kale, bok choy, etc.) - 6 cups, chopped
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger - 1 tablespoon, minced
  • Soy sauce or tamari - 2 tablespoons
  • Sesame oil - 1 tablespoon
  • Rice vinegar - 1 tablespoon
  • Sriracha or chili flakes - 1 teaspoon (optional, adjust to taste)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Oil - 2 tablespoons
  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and minced ginger. Stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add assorted greens to the pan. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until greens are wilted but still vibrant green.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce (or tamari), sesame oil, rice vinegar, sriracha (or chili flakes), and salt.
  5. Pour the sauce over the stir-fried greens. Toss well to coat evenly.
  6. Continue stir-frying for another 1-2 minutes until the greens are tender and the sauce has thickened slightly.
  7. Remove from heat.
  8. Adjust seasoning to taste.
  9. Serve hot as a side dish or over steamed rice. Enjoy!
