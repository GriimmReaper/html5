Red Beans and Rice Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Red kidney beans - 1 lb (about 2 cups), dried
  • Andouille sausage - 12 oz, sliced
  • Onion - 1 large, chopped
  • Celery - 2 stalks, chopped
  • Green bell pepper - 1, chopped
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Bay leaves - 2
  • Thyme - 1 tsp, dried
  • Cajun seasoning - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - 1 tsp, or to taste
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp, freshly ground
  • Chicken broth - 4 cups
  • Long-grain white rice - 2 cups, cooked
  • Green onions - for garnish
  1. Rinse the dried red kidney beans and soak them overnight in water.
  2. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the sliced Andouille sausage and cook until browned.
  3. Add chopped onion, celery, green bell pepper, and minced garlic to the pot. Saut√© until vegetables are softened.
  4. Drain and rinse the soaked beans, then add them to the pot along with bay leaves, dried thyme, Cajun seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Stir well.
  5. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 1.5 to 2 hours, stirring occasionally, until beans are tender and creamy.
  6. While the beans are simmering, cook the long-grain white rice according to package instructions.
  7. Once the beans are cooked and creamy, remove bay leaves and adjust seasoning if needed.
  8. Serve the red beans over cooked rice, garnished with chopped green onions.
