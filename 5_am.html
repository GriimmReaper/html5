Herbed Veggie Skillet Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Zucchini - 2 medium, sliced
  • Yellow squash - 2 medium, sliced
  • Red bell pepper - 1 large, sliced
  • Green bell pepper - 1 large, sliced
  • Red onion - 1 large, sliced
  • Cherry tomatoes - 1 cup
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Olive oil - 3 tbsp
  • Fresh thyme - 1 tbsp, chopped
  • Fresh rosemary - 1 tbsp, chopped
  • Salt - 1 tsp, or to taste
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp, freshly ground
  • Grated Parmesan cheese - for garnish (optional)
  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced zucchini, yellow squash, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, and red onion to the skillet. Season with salt and black pepper.
  4. Sauté the vegetables, stirring occasionally, until they are tender and slightly caramelized, about 8-10 minutes.
  5. Add cherry tomatoes, chopped fresh thyme, and chopped fresh rosemary to the skillet. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until tomatoes are heated through.
  6. Remove from heat and adjust seasoning if needed.
  7. Serve hot, optionally garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.
