Mexican Rice Recipe

Ingredients Instructions Image
  • Long grain rice - 1 cup, rinsed and drained
  • Vegetable oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Onion - 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
  • Tomato paste - 2 tablespoons
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium, diced
  • Vegetable or chicken broth - 1 3/4 cups
  • Ground cumin - 1 teaspoon
  • Chili powder - 1/2 teaspoon (adjust to taste)
  • Salt - to taste
  • Fresh cilantro - 2 tablespoons, chopped (optional, for garnish)
  • Lime wedges - for serving (optional)
  1. In a medium saucepan, heat vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onions and minced garlic. Cook until onions are softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add rinsed rice to the saucepan. Stir frequently and cook until rice is lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in tomato paste, diced tomatoes, vegetable or chicken broth, ground cumin, chili powder, and salt.
  5. Bring mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.
  6. Remove from heat and let the rice sit, covered, for 5 minutes.
  7. Fluff rice with a fork. Adjust seasoning if needed.
  8. Garnish with chopped cilantro, if desired.
  9. Serve hot with lime wedges on the side. Enjoy your Mexican Rice!
